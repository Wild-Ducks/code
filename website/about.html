At Wild Ducks, we believe technology should empower everyone—not just big corporations with deep pockets. Our mission is simple: use AI to help individuals, small businesses, and university departments overcome cost and complexity barriers, making advanced technology more accessible and practical.

Breaking Down Barriers ▼

Consider the process of patenting an invention. In 1807, Eli Whitney, a farm laborer and teacher, secured a patent for the cotton gin. Today, the cost of obtaining a patent can soar to $10,000–$20,000, making it nearly impossible for independent inventors to afford. That’s where AI steps in.

By automating tedious tasks—like formatting a patent to meet USPTO standards—AI can cut costs by a factor of ten while improving quality. This isn’t just a win for inventors; lawyers, too, can focus on higher-value work instead of drowning in paperwork.

But patents are just the beginning. Wild Ducks harnesses AI for content creation, risk assessments, structured searches (for jobs or products), marketing, and social media automation—eliminating repetitive work so people can focus on what they love.

Wild Ducks thrive by challenging the status quo—pushing boundaries, questioning assumptions, and creating new possibilities. That’s exactly what we do.
